1.About This Document

PRAXIS CONSULTING LTDA values your privacy. This document describes the types of personal information we handle when you use our website, how that information is used, and the rights available to you under applicable law.

2.Data We Collect

Depending on how you interact with our platform, we may gather:

  • Identification data: name, username, or similar identifiers

  • Marketing preferences: your choices regarding promotional communications

We handle your personal data only when lawfully permitted. Common purposes include:

4.Security Measures

We maintain technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. Access is limited to personnel and partners who require it for legitimate business purposes.

5.How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal data only for as long as needed to achieve the purposes described in this policy, including compliance with legal or accounting obligations. Retention periods are defined based on the nature and sensitivity of the data involved.

6.Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may exercise the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your data
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities

  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based

7.Cookies

We use cookies to distinguish visitors and enhance browsing experience. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser, though disabling them may affect site functionality.
